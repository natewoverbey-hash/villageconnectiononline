Where I Work & Live

The Old Village: Mount Pleasant's most coveted address

Bounded by Shem Creek and the Charleston Harbor, the Old Village and Old Mount Pleasant aren't subdivisions — they're a true neighborhood. No HOA. No gated entries. Historic homes, walkable streets, community gathering places, and a Lowcountry lifestyle that people spend years searching for.

🏛️

Historic character

Updated historic homes maintaining their original architecture — from 19th-century cottages to custom new construction built to honor the neighborhood's heritage.

🚶

Walkable to everything

Shem Creek waterfront restaurants, the Pitt Street Bridge trail, Alhambra Hall, and the beloved Pitt Street Pharmacy are all steps away.

⚓

Lowcountry waterfront life

Minutes from Sullivan's Island beaches, Downtown Charleston, and the waterways of the Charleston Harbor. Boat ramps, marsh views, and stunning sunsets.

📈

A market that holds its value

Limited inventory, loyal buyer demand, and enduring community appeal make the Old Village one of Charleston's most resilient real estate markets.

🤝

Real community life

Car parties. Pickleball leagues. The Cooper River Bridge Run. Mt. Pleasant Christmas Parade. Old Village residents don't just own homes here — they belong here.

📍

Unbeatable location

Minutes to Downtown Charleston, top beaches, top-rated schools, and major medical centers — yet tucked away enough to feel like your own private corner of the Lowcountry.

What makes Old Village real estate different from other Mount Pleasant neighborhoods?

+

The Old Village isn't a subdivision — it's a true, organic community. No HOA. Historic character. Walkable to Shem Creek and the harbor. It attracts a loyal buyer pool who wants authentic Lowcountry living without the cookie-cutter feel. That's what makes it hold its value so well.

How is the Old Village real estate market right now?

+

Healthy but more nuanced than 2023–2024. Prices have held firm — at the high end they've continued to climb — but buyers are more selective. Well-priced, well-presented homes still move quickly. Reach out and I'll give you a current read specific to your street or property type.
